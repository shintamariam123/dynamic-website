The confectionery

The Studio

We invite you to our own Karmel & Celcil confectionery studio, located in the charming area of Ustanów near Warsaw. We are specialists in creating sophisticated petit gateau - handmade cookies based on chocolate and flavored fruit additives, as well as in monores, such as tarpauses, macaroons, without and eclergy. Our offer also includes cakes and craft ice creams, which we make on request throughout the year. We cordially invite you to our intimate cafe, where in an elegant atmosphere you can enjoy exquisite tastes and rest. Carmel & Chocolate is an ideal place for lovers of confectionery and a sophisticated kitchen.

The Offer

In our studio we offer unique flavors that you will not find in ordinary stores. Our offer is addressed to both individual clients and companies. We will be happy to prepare a sweet table for you for all circumstances, so that guests can enjoy the unique flavors and unforgettable sensations. All our products are made of the highest quality ingredients, ensuring that the taste and appearance are perfect. Give us a chance to offer you the best taste that will remain in your memory for a long time.
